My Work

This is work from a old D&AD creative brief. The brief asked participants to connect a brand to technology to create something not possible or thought of 3 years ago.

After sitting down with the brief and brainstorming for quite sometime, my partner and I came up with the idea of UnHinged.

Essentially it is the breakup mode made to be turned off from the dating app designed to be deleted. We thought if Hinge's Noble-Prize winning algorithim can help pair you with your perfect match, then it can help you find a breakup buddy too.
