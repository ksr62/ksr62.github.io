Chivas Regal Brief

Chivas Regal tasked us with creating the blended Scotch Whiskey of tomorrow for the next generation of hustlers.

It was vital to the brief that we considered the product concept, packaging and identiy, as well as product launch all while making our bottle a status symbol.

And thus, Chivas Regal XIX was born, a perfect blend of sweet and smoky that embodies the forgotten generation of Hyrbid Hustler's success.

Chivas Regal Commercial Script

It took a global pandemic to shed a light on this forgotten generation of hustlers. The ones who telecommunicated for reasons far related to a pandemic. The ones who did it just to prove it could be done.

No time is unprecedented for these pioneers. They brought Wall Street to their street before the world knew it to be possible. They are trailblazers. Innovators. Visionaries. They are hybrid hustlers.

And so, we crafted a blend to celebrate them Introducing Chivas Regal XIX, infused with the aromatic notes of vanilla and honeyed apples, topped with a mellow smokiness to give our hustlers a luxuriously, long finish. Sweet and smoky, the perfect hybrid to embody our hustlers’ success.

Crafted with blended whiskies originating from distilleries no longer in existence, Chivas Regal XIX is a success sought after. Designed to adorn every office, everywhere and provide the perfect sip to clock out the day. So let your bottle become a mark of grandeur and cheers to the ones who kept the good times rolling.

Connect4Climate Brief

This work is also from a previous D&AD Creative Brief for Connect4Climate where we were asked to create a guerrilla marketing campaign that encourages sports fans to take action against climate change.

My partner and I had the idea to take AR technology currently being used in college football stadiums to "flood" Hard Rock Stadium, home of the Miami Hurricanes. This Flooded Fields Campaign is intended to spark conversation and encourage fans to take action to protect the fate of college football and our future.

Connect4Climate Execution Copy

What if we provided fans with a mixed-reality experience highlighting how the fate of college football is not in the distant future, but instead much closer than we think? Using augmented reality with live rendition videos, we will flood Hard Rock Stadium.

Before the Miami Hurricanes rush the field, fans will be advised via a video on the jumbotron to take out their phones, turn on their camera, and point it in the direction of the team tunnel. The stadium lights will flicker, flood sirens will wail, and the Miami Hurricanes will emerge from AR flood waters to Kiss’ “All Hell’s Breakin Loose” in order to save the Miami Hurricanes and Hard Rock Stadium, fans will be encouraged to donate to a link embedded in the Sport4Climate website that will appear on their phones.

The more fans donate, the more the flood waters will recede. Fans can watch the progression of the flood at any point in the game by pulling out their phones and activating their cameras.
